# Persona: The Channel Partner / Systems Integrator

**Who they are:** Big consultancies (PwC, Accenture, Deloitte, KPMG) and vertical systems integrators (JAKALA, Turing, TribeAI) who implement or resell Claude into their own enterprise client base. A secondary, B2B2B persona — a channel/partner relationship as much as an end-customer one.

## Pain points & use cases
- Needs to train and certify large internal teams on Claude/Claude Code to operationalize it at scale for clients, not just adopt it themselves.
- Wants co-build and joint-GTM economics — industry-specific accelerators rather than a standard end-user product relationship.
- Acts as an implementation layer for regulated-industry clients (e.g., named as financial-services implementation partners).

## Messaging hooks Anthropic uses
- No single consumer-style headline tagline (unlike the other six personas) — messaging here is delivered through partner-program and partnership-announcement channels rather than a solutions-page hero line.
- Tone: co-build/joint-GTM economics, industry-specific accelerators — not direct end-user messaging.
- Proof points: PwC trained 400 consultants on Claude Code; $100M Claude Partner Network investment; Partner Hub / Services Track launch.
