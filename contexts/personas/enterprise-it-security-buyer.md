# Persona: The Enterprise IT/Security Buyer

**Who they are:** CIOs, CISOs, IT/procurement, and governance leads at large companies. This is the **economic buyer**, distinct from the end user — the person who signs off on rollout, not the person typing prompts day to day. Reflects a classic champion/user vs. economic-buyer dual-audience enterprise sales motion.

## Pain points & use cases
- Needs governed, auditable AI access across an organization, not ad-hoc individual chat usage.
- Cares about data residency, audit policy, security certifications, and procurement/security review processes before approving a rollout.
- High-stakes work that has to hold up to scrutiny — legal, financial, or regulatory exposure if AI output can't be explained or traced.
