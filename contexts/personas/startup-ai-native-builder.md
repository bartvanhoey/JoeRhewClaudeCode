# Persona: The Startup / AI-Native Product Builder

**Who they are:** Early-stage, often VC-backed founders building AI-native products — frequently building ON Claude as infrastructure (via the API/platform), not just using it as an internal chat tool. Eligibility for Anthropic's startup program skews toward venture-backed, early-stage companies (founded within ~4 years), though it's framed as open to non-VC-backed founders too.

## Pain points & use cases
- Needs to move fast to product-market fit with limited runway — cost and rate-limit friction matter more here than for large enterprises.
- Wants non-dilutive support (credits, priority access) and a peer community, not just a product purchase.
- Often building a product whose core value proposition *is* Claude under the hood — these companies double as proof points of Anthropic's platform/API strength.

## Messaging hooks Anthropic uses
- *"Build and break through."* — Startups program tagline. (https://claude.com/programs/startups)
- *"Join the founders building on Claude."*
- Tone: scrappy, momentum/growth-focused, community-oriented — credits and founder events, not governance language.

## Representative solutions page / case studies
- **Claude for Startups** program page (https://claude.com/programs/startups) is the direct representation of this persona.
- Case studies: Cursor, Lovable (*"Create software 20x faster with Claude"*), Replit, Warp, Juno (chronic-illness symptom tracking), delight.ai (internal tools via Claude Code), ChatPlace ("AI marketing team for solo creators").
