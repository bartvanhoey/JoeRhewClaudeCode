# Anthropic Persona Archetypes

Seven inferred persona archetypes synthesized from Anthropic's public-facing segmentation, role language, and case studies. **Not an official Anthropic framework** — see `research/personas.md` for full sourcing and caveats.

- **[builder-developer.md](builder-developer.md)** — Software engineers and technical founders who use Claude Code to ship faster; Anthropic's largest real-world usage base.
- **[enterprise-it-security-buyer.md](enterprise-it-security-buyer.md)** — CIOs/CISOs and procurement/security reviewers who approve governed, auditable Claude rollouts as the economic buyer.
- **[regulated-industry-risk-owner.md](regulated-industry-risk-owner.md)** — Compliance, legal, actuarial, and clinical-affairs staff in finance, healthcare, life sciences, legal, and government who need defensible, auditable AI output.
- **[knowledge-worker-non-technical-operator.md](knowledge-worker-non-technical-operator.md)** — Analysts, ops, marketing, and finance staff who handle documents but don't code, served primarily by Claude Cowork.
- **[startup-ai-native-builder.md](startup-ai-native-builder.md)** — Early-stage, often VC-backed founders building AI-native products on top of Claude as infrastructure.
- **[educator-institutional-steward.md](educator-institutional-steward.md)** — University faculty and administrators evaluating Claude for Education, with academic integrity as the central concern.
- **[channel-partner-systems-integrator.md](channel-partner-systems-integrator.md)** — Consultancies and SIs (PwC, JAKALA, etc.) that implement or resell Claude into their own enterprise client base.
