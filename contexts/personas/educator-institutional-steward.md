# Persona: The Educator / Institutional Steward

**Who they are:** University administrators, faculty, and ed-tech decision-makers — a distinct enough audience from the general enterprise buyer to warrant its own tone and product features. Claude for Education launched April 2025, targeted at higher-ed institutions.

## Pain points & use cases
- Worried about academic integrity and "cognitive atrophy" — the core anxiety this persona brings to any AI adoption conversation.
- Needs an institutional procurement motion (not an individual-user motion): integration with existing campus systems like Canvas/Instructure.
- Three distinct named audiences with separate value props on the same page: students, educators/faculty, administrators.

## Messaging hooks Anthropic uses
- *"Navigating AI in education together."* / *"Claude helps universities maintain academic integrity while incorporating AI tools in education, with Anthropic's commitment to safety."* (https://claude.com/solutions/education)
- *"A thinking partner — not answer machine."*
- "Learning Mode" product feature built specifically to address pedagogical concerns (guides reasoning rather than supplying answers).
- Tone: safety and pedagogy first, productivity second — the opposite emphasis order from the developer/startup tracks, and (along with Legal) one of the only two tracks where Anthropic proactively pre-empts skepticism in its headline copy rather than leading with capability.
