## 4. Market Share / Third-Party Data Points

- **Ramp AI Index (May 2026)** — real corporate card/invoice data across 50,000+ US companies: Claude leads US business AI adoption at **34.4%** vs. ChatGPT's **32.3%** — first time Anthropic has led this dataset. Anthropic reportedly quadrupled its adoption share over the prior year.
- **Menlo Ventures, "2025 Mid-Year LLM Market Update"** (150 technical leaders surveyed + spend analysis) — Anthropic captured **32%** of enterprise LLM usage in production vs. OpenAI's 25% (down from 50% in 2023) and Google's 20%. Enterprise LLM spend more than doubled, $3.5B (Nov 2024) → $8.4B (mid-2025).
- **Coding-specific share** (Menlo Ventures) — Anthropic ~54% vs. OpenAI ~21% of the enterprise coding-model market — Anthropic's strongest wedge.
- **Closed- vs. open-source** (Menlo Ventures) — closed-source models now power 87% of enterprise workloads (vs. 13% open-source, down from 19%).
- **Claude Code revenue** — reached $2.5B annualized revenue by February 2026 (per press roundups of Anthropic disclosures), cited as a primary driver of enterprise coding share gains.

*Note: Ramp and Menlo figures are third-party/press-sourced, not Anthropic-published — solid for directional claims, re-verify exact numbers against primary sources before quoting in customer-facing materials.*
